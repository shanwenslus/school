School Engagment services

We provide specialized services for students who experience significant anxiety surrounding school attendance. These services involve a highly individualized program to help the student engage in school. individualized services may include a modified day with a plan for systematic desensitization to increase school attendance, alternate learning options, and highly individualized behavioral programming.
